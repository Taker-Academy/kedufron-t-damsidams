    Account Termination Policy

    Procedure

    Infraction Consequences

    Any violation or infraction of the rules outlined in our policies may result in the immediate termination of your account and services with DirtyCloud.
    The decision to terminate accounts will be made at the discretion of the DirtyCloud team and is final.

    Notification

    DirtyCloud may, but is not obligated to, provide notice of account termination.
    However, in cases of severe policy violations or security concerns, immediate termination may occur without prior notification.

    Appeals

    Users have the right to appeal an account termination by contacting our support team on Telegram.
    Appeals will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis, and DirtyCloud reserves the right to accept or deny any appeal.

    How Can I Get Ban?

    Any form of DDoS attack is strictly prohibited
    (all type of layer attack).

    In accordance with our policies, we strictly prohibit the targeting of users located in Russia or in CIS related country.
    Violations of this rule will result in immediate termination of services, without prior notice or warning.

    Hosting, engaging in, or facilitating any activities related to child and animal abuse or terrorism on our servers is strictly forbidden.

    The intentional overloading of DirtyCloud's network infrastructure is strictly prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to, activities such as excessive bandwidth consumption, Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks, or any other actions that significantly degrade network performance.

    Network spoofing, in any form, is strictly forbidden. Spoofing involves manipulating network packets or data to deceive or mislead our network systems or other users. This includes source IP address spoofing, MAC address spoofing, or any other method used to falsify network identity.
